Features

  • Construct and customize your own graphs, which can be directed or undirected, weighted or unweighted.
  • Simulate algorithms on your customized graphs, step by step, and watch the progress of the algorithm live.
  • Export your graphs either as images, or as GraphML files that Graphy understands.
  • Customize Graphy for you or your institute, with additional algorithms, graph traversals, or anything else you'd like to see Graphy simulate step by step. Just get in touch!
