Constructing Graphs

Click, drag, and construct your graphs; be they directed, undirected, weighted, unweighted, or any valid permutation of the four.

Modifying Edges

A simple right click allows you to either give your edges some weight and capacity, or wipe them off the face of the graph.

Simulating Algorithms

Select from an extensive list of algorithms to simulate on your graph, step by step. You could either run them manually or go hands free.

Saving To GraphML

Save and share your most complex graphs as simple, portable GraphML files that Graphy understands.

Exporting Graphs

Easily export your graphs as JPEGs and embed them in your project or research papers.
